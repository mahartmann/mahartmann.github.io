Mareike Hartmann

Hi, I am Mareike, a PostDoc at the Computational Linguistics Group of the Department of Language Science and Technology at Saarland University.

My current research interests are LLM-based agents interacting with external environments and augmented LLMs.

Before joining UdS, I worked as a researcher at the Department for Interactive Machine Learning at DFKI, and obtained a PhD from the University of Copenhagen.
